Purpose

The purpose of the website is to position the brand in the market, become more accessible to customers, increase sales, reduce advertising costs and have more credibility in the business.

Audience

Question: Who are the target customers?
Answer: Anyone interested in rafting, sports, exercising, entities interested in team building

Question: What do they want?
Answer:Adrenaline, satisfaction, fun, desire to break out of a sedentary lifestyle and routine, team building activities and sports fitness

Question: How will my audience usually access my site?
Answer: Mobile device, desktop, laptop.
